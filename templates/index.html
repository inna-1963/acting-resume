Acting Career Chronicle

Over the years I have been in many productions. Generations, Majakathata, Ekasi Stories and films which aired on Mzansi Magic.

On Generations I played the character Mavis(Feature) Choppa's Maid in 2014, and also in 2015 MamGubeni(Feature) Gogo Flo's society member.

In Majakathata I played a featuring role for that episode, I played Sister Mantoa the partner of the lead. This was in 2015 on SABC2.

Ekasi stories was a ETV franchise, I worked on two projects Street Justice(2012), where I was MaZondi(Lead) and Skroef no Sexy(2015), where I was Angie Moloi(Feature).

Currently on Mzansi Magic there are projects like Isithandwa Sam, I played MaKhumalo(Lead) in 2018. Imfihlo, I played MaDlamini(Lead) in 2019. Izingane Zami, I played Gogo Lydia(Lead) in 2020 to name a few.
